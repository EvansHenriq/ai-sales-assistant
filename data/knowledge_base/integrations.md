# FlowMetrics — Integrations & Security

## Integrations
- SDKs: JavaScript/TypeScript, Python, Go, iOS (Swift) and Android (Kotlin).
- Data warehouse export: Snowflake, BigQuery and Amazon Redshift.
- Reverse ETL / messaging: Segment, Slack and webhooks.
- Authentication: SSO via SAML and OIDC (Scale and Enterprise plans).

## Security & compliance
- Encryption in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256).
- SOC 2 Type II report available under NDA.
- GDPR-ready: data processing agreement, EU data residency, and PII minimization.
- Role-based access control and audit logs.

## Data residency
Customers on Scale and Enterprise can choose EU or US data residency. Enterprise
can also self-host within their own cloud account.
