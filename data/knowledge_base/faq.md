# FlowMetrics — FAQ

## How long does setup take?
Most teams capture their first events within an hour using the SDKs and the
guided onboarding checklist.

## Can we try it before buying?
Yes. There is a free Starter plan and a 14-day free trial of the Growth plan with
no credit card required.

## Do you support single sign-on (SSO)?
SSO via SAML and OIDC is available on the Scale and Enterprise plans.

## How is pricing calculated?
By tracked monthly events, not per seat. Team members and dashboards are
unlimited on every plan.

## Where is data stored?
Starter and Growth run in the US by default. Scale and Enterprise can choose EU
or US data residency; Enterprise can self-host.

## Do you offer onboarding help?
Growth includes email support; Scale adds priority support; Enterprise includes a
dedicated onboarding specialist and an SLA.
