# FlowMetrics — Product Overview

FlowMetrics is a B2B SaaS product-analytics platform that helps software teams
understand how users adopt and engage with their products. It is fictional and
used here to demonstrate a retrieval-augmented sales assistant.

## What it does
- Event tracking: capture product events from web, mobile and backend via SDKs.
- Funnels & retention: visualize conversion funnels and cohort retention.
- Dashboards: build and share dashboards across teams.
- Alerts: get notified when key metrics move beyond thresholds.

## Who it is for
Product managers, growth teams and engineers at companies from early-stage
startups to mid-market SaaS (typically 20–500 employees).

## Key differentiators
- Fast time-to-value: SDKs and a guided setup get teams to first insight in under
  an hour.
- Privacy-first: EU and US data residency options; PII minimization by default.
- Transparent pricing based on tracked monthly events, not seats.
