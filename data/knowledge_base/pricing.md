# FlowMetrics — Pricing

Pricing is based on tracked monthly events (not per-seat). All plans include
unlimited dashboards and unlimited team members.

## Plans
- **Starter — $0/month**: up to 10,000 monthly events, 1 project, 30-day data
  retention, community support.
- **Growth — $99/month**: up to 1,000,000 monthly events, 5 projects, 12-month
  retention, email support, alerts.
- **Scale — $499/month**: up to 10,000,000 monthly events, unlimited projects,
  24-month retention, priority support, SSO/SAML, data residency choice.
- **Enterprise — custom**: volume above 10M events, custom retention, dedicated
  support, security review, SLA, and self-hosting options.

## Billing notes
- Monthly or annual billing; annual saves ~17%.
- Overages on events are billed at $1 per additional 100,000 events.
- A 14-day free trial of the Growth plan is available; no credit card required.
